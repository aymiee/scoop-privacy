Scoop - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

Scoop is a video frame extraction app. Your privacy is simple: we don't collect anything.

Data Collection

Scoop does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

Photos and Videos

Scoop accesses your photo library and files only when you choose to import a video or save a frame. All processing happens locally on your device. No videos, images, or files are ever uploaded to any server.

Analytics

Scoop does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

Scoop does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Scoop does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
