Scoop - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

Scoop is a video frame extraction and audio stem separation app. Your privacy is simple: we don't collect anything. Scoop does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data, including personal information, usage data, device identifiers, or diagnostics. All processing occurs entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Scoop does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Scoop does not store or retain any user data.

Photos and Videos

Scoop accesses your photo library and files only when you choose to import a video or save a frame. All processing happens locally on your device. No videos, images, or files are uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

Audio Processing

Scoop can separate audio from videos into individual stems (Voice, Music, Bass, and SFX) using on-device machine learning (CoreML). All audio processing runs entirely on your device. No audio data is uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

Share Extension

Scoop includes an iOS Share Extension that allows you to send videos to the app directly from other apps (e.g., Safari, Photos, or messaging apps). Content shared via the iOS Share Extension is processed locally on your device and is never stored or transmitted by Scoop.

Analytics

Scoop does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

Scoop does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Scoop does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Scoop - Support

About Scoop

Scoop gives you two powerful tools for any video:

Frame Scoop — Pull any frame from a video and save it as a photo. Scrub through frame by frame until you find the perfect one, then save or share it instantly.

Audio Scoop — Strip out unwanted audio from a video. Scoop separates voice, music, bass, and sound effects into individual layers. Mute what you don't want and save a clean version.

Supported Formats

MP4, MOV, M4V — any video iOS can play. Audio separation supports videos up to 30 seconds.
